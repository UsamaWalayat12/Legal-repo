================================================================================
SAMPLE RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT (For Demo Purposes Only)
================================================================================

This Residential Lease Agreement is made on February 1, 2024 between
Landlord (Lessor) and Tenant (Lessee).

1. PROPERTY
   The landlord agrees to rent the property located at 123 Main Street,
   Apartment 4B, Anytown, USA.

2. TERM
   The lease term shall be twelve (12) months, commencing on March 1, 2024
   and ending on February 28, 2025.

3. RENT
   Monthly rent is $1,500.00, payable on the first day of each month.
   A late fee of $50.00 applies if rent is not received by the 5th day of
   the month.

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT
   A security deposit of $1,500.00 is required upon signing. The deposit
   shall be returned within 30 days of lease termination, less any deductions
   for damages beyond normal wear and tear.

5. UTILITIES
   Tenant is responsible for electricity, gas, and internet. Landlord is
   responsible for water and trash collection.

6. PET POLICY
   No pets are allowed without prior written consent of the landlord.
   A pet deposit of $300.00 may be required if approved.

7. NOTICE PERIOD
   Either party must provide thirty (30) days written notice for
   termination or non-renewal of the lease.
