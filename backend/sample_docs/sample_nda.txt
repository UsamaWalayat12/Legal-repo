================================================================================
SAMPLE MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (For Demo Purposes Only)
================================================================================

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of
January 1, 2024 by and between Party A and Party B.

1. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
   "Confidential Information" means any information disclosed by one party to
   the other, either directly or indirectly, in writing, orally, or by drawings
   or observation, that is marked as confidential or would reasonably be
   understood to be confidential given the nature of the information.

2. OBLIGATIONS
   The receiving party shall:
   (a) Maintain the confidentiality of the Confidential Information;
   (b) Not disclose such information to any third party without prior written
       consent;
   (c) Use the Confidential Information only for the purpose of evaluating a
       potential business relationship.

3. TERM
   This Agreement shall survive for a period of five (5) years from the date
   of disclosure.

4. EXCLUSIONS
   Confidential Information does not include information that:
   (a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving party;
   (b) Was rightfully in the receiving party's possession prior to disclosure;
   (c) Is independently developed by the receiving party without use of the
       Confidential Information.

5. GOVERNING LAW
   This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
